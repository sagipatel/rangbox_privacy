Google AdMob & Advertising

RangBox uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use your device's advertising identifier and other data to serve personalized ads.

  • Ads displayed include: Banner, Interstitial, Rewarded, Native, and App Open ads
  • Google may use cookies and similar technologies to improve ad relevance
  • You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings
Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking → disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"

For more information, see the Google Advertising Policy.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore), which is hosted on Google Cloud infrastructure with industry-standard encryption at rest and in transit.

While we implement commercially reasonable security measures, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.
